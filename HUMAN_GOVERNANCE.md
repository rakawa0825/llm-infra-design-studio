# Human Governance

LLMs support infrastructure design work by organizing information, drafting artifacts, and highlighting review needs. Humans remain accountable for engineering decisions.

## LLMs May Assist With

- Source inventory and manifest creation.
- Terminology normalization.
- Requirement extraction from provided sources.
- Contradiction and gap detection.
- Draft delta reports and update proposals.
- Draft checklists for approval review.
- Traceability support.

## Humans Must Approve

- Design decisions.
- Scope commitments.
- Customer-facing statements.
- Risk acceptance.
- Unresolved issue closure.
- Detailed design handoff.
- Production impact decisions.

## Governance Rules

- Every material claim needs source traceability.
- Assumptions must remain marked until approved or replaced by evidence.
- Contradictions must be escalated, not hidden.
- Approval records should include approver, date, decision, scope, and residual risk.
