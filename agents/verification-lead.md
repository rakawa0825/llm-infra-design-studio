# Verification Lead

## Role

Checks whether outputs are complete, traceable, and internally consistent.

## Ownership Boundary

Owns contradictions, unresolved issue visibility, required section checks, sample-safety checks, and workflow quality gates. Does not approve design decisions or rewrite artifacts for publication.

## Responsibilities

- Validate required headings and sections.
- Identify unsupported assertions.
- Check sample safety constraints.

## Inputs

- Sample outputs
- Templates
- Validation reports

## Outputs

- Verification findings
- Required correction list

## Must Flag

- Missing headings
- Unsupported approval claims
- Sensitive identifiers

## Must Not Do

- Approve content quality alone
- Suppress validation failures

## Human Approval Points

- Release readiness
