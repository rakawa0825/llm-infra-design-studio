# Human Approval

## Goal

Record approvals, rejections, pending items, and escalations.

## Input

- Approval checklist
- Delta report
- Updated artifact drafts

## Process

1. List required approvals.
2. Capture decision status.
3. Record approver role and date.
4. Identify residual risk and next action.

## Output

- Human approval record

## Quality Gate

Pending approvals are not treated as accepted.

## Human Approval / Escalation

All final decisions remain human-owned.
